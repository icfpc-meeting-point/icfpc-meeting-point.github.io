Meeting Point - About

What is it

Can't find team to join? Need more team mates? Announce here.

How

Click on map in the city where you need to link with people and create single-person team first. Someone will find and contact you. Or maybe there's already a team.

Why?
  • Because it's difficult to locate local people to form local team. While it's ok to cooperate via skype, it's still better to meet in person.
  • Because there's no central place to find info about teams after contest has ended. Probably, teams will leave something about themselves here.
Two on-site people are better than those three over skype!
